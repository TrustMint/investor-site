Market Opportunity

The biotech funding market ($2.5T) is inefficient and closed to most investors. We use Web3 and AI to create a new, more liquid and transparent market.

Problem: Locked Capital and High Barriers

Breakthrough scientific developments require huge investments but suffer from illiquidity (capital frozen for 5-10 years) and information asymmetry. This makes the market accessible only to elite funds.

Our Solution: Infrastructure for Growth

  • RWA Tokenization: We turn future royalties from patents into liquid digital assets (IP-tokens) on the BNB Chain, opening access to retail and institutional investors.
  • AI Scoring: Our AI "Prometheus" analyzes risks, potential, and scientific validity of projects, providing unprecedented transparency.
  • Open Marketplace: We are creating a decentralized marketplace where scientists get funding directly, and investors get diversified access to a new asset class (DeSci).
